# Airport Passenger Traffic Bar Chart

This bar chart shows the passenger numbers at some of the busiest airports in the world in 2026. Atlanta has the highest passenger traffic with about 108 million passengers. Dubai is second with around 92 million passengers. Tokyo Haneda and London Heathrow also have many travelers, showing they are important international airports. Hong Kong International Airport has the lowest number in this chart with about 53 million passengers, but it is still one of the world’s busiest airports. 

The chart helps people understand global travel and tourism. Airports with more passengers usually have more flights, business travel, and international visitors.
